- ANTMAPS -

ANTMAPS is a web-based application to visualize the GABI database. There are three views the user can select, each visualizing a different aspect of ant biodiversity. The Diversity View provides an overview of the total ant species richness in each region as well as the subfamily and genus diversity in each region . The Species Distribution View visualizes individual species distributions and its status (native etc.) in each region. The Region Comparison View displays the overlapping fauna between a selected region and every other region.
Click on the buttons in the side bar to switch views.
